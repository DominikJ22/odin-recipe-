Waffles Recipe

Description

Do you want to eat a great breakfast meal. Here is a wonderful waffle recipe to eat in the morning.

Ingredients

  • 1 package of active dry yeast
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup warm milk
  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 1/2 white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons Vanilla extract
  • 4 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 3 egg whites

Steps

  1. Dissolve year in 1/4 cup of warm milk.
  2. Whisk together the egg yolk, 1/4 cup of warm milk, and melted butter.
  3. Stir the yeast, sugar,salt,and vanilla and combine it with the remaining milk and flour.
  4. Beat the egg whites and fold them into the batter.
  5. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and keep warm for 1 hour.
  6. Place batter onto waffle iron and cook until golden brown!
