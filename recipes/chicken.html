Fried Chicken Recipe

Description

Here is a tasty chicken recipe for dinner. Chicken is a classic food that taste good any day of the week.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of buttermilk mix
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried sage
  • 2 pounds of whole chicken
  • 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  • 1/3 teaspoon butter

Steps

  1. Preheat over to 350 degrees F and grease baking sheet.
  2. Combine all mix and seasoning into a dish.
  3. Dip the chicken in the evaporated milk and coat it in the mixed seasoning.
  4. Place in the baking dish and cover with butter.
  5. Bake the chicken for 1 hour.
