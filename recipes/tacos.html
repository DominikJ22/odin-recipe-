Tacos

Description

Here is a recipe for some tasty homemade tacos. These are some classic beef and bean tacos with some vegetables and are ready to serve to a family of four. I hope you all enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 cuo of shredded cheese
  • 1 taco season kit
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 6-12 FLour tortillas
  • shredded lettuce
  • 1 container of taco sauce

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef for 10-12 minutes on nonstick skillet or until brown.
  2. Add the water and taco seasoning.
  3. Cook on low heat until the water simmers.
  4. Place on tortilla shells and top it with your favorite toppings
